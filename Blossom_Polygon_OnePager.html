Blossom provides core banking for 4,500+ credit unions. Together we're building a full stablecoin offering that includes issuance powered by Brale and a Mastercard card program. Polygon will provide the regulated on/off-ramps and embedded wallet infrastructure with custodial and noncustodial optionality in one integration.
What Blossom Is Building — Where Polygon Fits
UC1

Stablecoin On/Off-Ramps

  • Coinme: 48 US state MTL coverage for on/off ramps which includes card, bank transfer and cash redemption at 50K+ cash locations incl. Walmart.
  • Set up a custodial float account or treasury account where we have access to the Blossom issued stablecoin liquidity and Blossom provides the capital. Draw down as needed.
  • Stablecoin coverage: deep USDC and USDT liquidity along with coverage for additional regulated stablecoins e.g. USDG, SOFID.
  • CU members can deposit USD → auto-convert to Blossom stablecoin.
  • Redeem stablecoin → USD back to CU or individual deposit account.
UC2

Embedded Wallets for CU Members

  • White-labeled wallets inside Blossom and CU partner applications.
  • Custodial + non-custodial models supported - issue as many custodial or non-custodial wallets as needed for respective CU's.
  • KYC'd through existing CU onboarding. FDIC category metadata per wallet.
  • Non-custodial: No seed phrases, no gas fees - invisible to members.
UC3

Stablecoin-Linked Cards

  • Blossom as program manager for CU-branded cards.
  • Stablecoin-funded card spend.
  • Polygon to power wallets and ramps.
UC4

P2P Payments

  • Member-to-member P2P in Blossom stablecoin - competing with Zelle.
  • Expansion to cross-border corridors: LatAm + Caribbean.
  • Inter-CU settlement: instant vs. T+1-3 on ACH.
  • Closed-loop lending and borrowing marketplace.
